iChromoto Settings

iChromoto is: A nicer, more useful, New Tab page for the Chrome web browser.


Domain Blacklists

You can provide a list of domain names, one per line, that you do not want iChromoto to take screenshots of or track. iChromoto will ignore any pages that fall under the blacklisted domains. You can use the "*" wildcard character to match sections of the domain name. For example:

  • example.com - Will ignore "example.com". Will not ignore "www.example.com".
  • *.example.com - Will ignore any subdomain of "example.com". Will not ignore "example.com".
  • www.example.com - Will ignore "www.example.com". Will not ignore "example.com" or "foo.example.com".
  • *.net - Will ignore any .net domain name.

Regular Expression Blacklists

URLs that match the patterns specified, one per line, will not be tracked and no screenshots will be collected. The patterns use regular expression syntax.
